Dataset

A comprehensive data set of deck a rankings and each respective card list.
This data is provided by MTGMelee and the SCG 5K Kaldheim Tournament. It contains all the tournament results for each player, the deck they played and the cards they played in the deck. This tournament contained 272 players, with 33 distinct deck and 351 dictinct cards. I have been a long fan of Magic the Gathering, so these visualizations are a gift to the community of players that want to better understand the current metagame and what decks/cards are performing well this season.

The data is available online at: https://mtgmelee.com/Tournament/View/5254

Initial Analysis Questions

  1. Do any Decks noticeably outperform others?
  2. Do any Cards noticeably outperform others?
  3. Is there a correlation between Win % and card selection?
